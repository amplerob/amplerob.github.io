Get Connected in a Small Group

UCC's small groups provide an opportunity for us to grow in our relationships with God and one another. These weekly gatherings include Bible study, sharing, and prayer (and sometimes cookies). Small groups meet at various times throughout the week all over the Uptown area. Check the list below for a group that suits you.
