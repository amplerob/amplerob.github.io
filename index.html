I’m Rob. I do stuff.

Minor works on display here – a graphic designer’s lightening round. I work at Ample designing websites and apps, creating prototypes, overseeing UX and committing front-end code.

And when I’m not behind a monitor…I wander the wilderness, mentor at a local men’s shelter, help wrangle a Boy Scout Troop, facilitate honest conversations about life and faith, yell at the Buffalo Bills, drink beer (occasionally my own) and play a cattywampus style of ping pong.

I live in Bend, Oregon. My work is in Cincinnati, Ohio. I’m an owner of two dogs, father of two kids and husband of one amazing lady.
