Backstory

This local hangout is just as dedicated to community as it is to coffee. And now their identity is off and galloping (or whatever goats do) — a dream come to true for the eager new owner.

In the end, I created concept sketches, the final logo and the makings of a branding deck. But if I were on the hook for more…the ornery nature of the goat is too irresistible. Imagine large format vinyl wall decals throughout the shop. Hooves in high places. A head creeping out of nook or corner. Off kilter crops. Oh the possibilities.

If I don't know you, I'd like to. Wait. Is that creepy?
